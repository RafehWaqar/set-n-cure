How preservation works

From your parcel to your shelf

Sending flowers or keepsakes to preserve? Here's the order things happen in.

01

You send or drop off

Pack your fresh flowers or item securely and send them our way, or drop them off if you're local.

02

We dry, arrange & pour

Each piece is press-dried, arranged by hand, then sealed in resin and cured for 24–48 hours.

03

We finish & deliver

Sanded, polished and packed with care, then shipped to your door within 15–18 days.

Our story

From our hearts to your home

Amber & Bloom started on a kitchen table with a bag of resin and a bunch of roses that didn't want to be thrown away. Every plaque, jhumka and clock we make since has carried that same instinct: nothing worth keeping should have to fade.
